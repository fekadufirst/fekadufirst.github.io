Site Purpose Statement

We intends to provide accurate, accessible, and reliable weather service or information about the weather condition for every town, city, states, country, and continent Services purpose is providing excellent service for the customers and make their experience unforgettable. Our goal is to make our website service to become the recognized local and global information source with an easy, handy, and useful information.

Our service is great destination for weather on a county-by-county basis in the United States. By typing in your city and state, or just your zip code, and you'll get a detailed forecast for where you are, or alternatively, where you're headed out to. Current conditions, future predictions, long-term forecasts, historical highs and lows, precipitation chances, radar and satellite maps, and more are included. This service is proved for anyone who is looking for weather services and information.

The Target Audience

My target famers, fishers, hunters, gardeners, homeowners, travelers, aviation (airlines), landscaper, and hikers. Our website is for anyone who enjoy outdoor sports and activities. It is for anyone who needs the weather information to do necessary task and perform daily duties. Without age and education criteria, we provide this weather service and build this website for anyone who can read and understand forecast data.

Our service and website will be accessible in any electronics devices. However, we focus more to meet and fit the needs and concerns of out mobile users and customers. Therefore, using smart phones to access our services is possible and handy wherever you maybe. Anyone can access our website by trying the name.
