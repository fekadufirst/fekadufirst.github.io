Running Shoes Site Plan

Purpose Statement

The project intends to provide durable, comfortable, stylish running shoes for both men and women with affordable price. Our goal is to make our website service to become the recognized local and global running shoes business center with an easy, handy, and useful information. We offer services like reserving and delivering orders in cities wherever we have storage.

Audience

Potential Clients

My target audiences are athletes, sport clubs, runners, physical education centers, and sport funs. Our website is for anyone who enjoy outdoor sports and activities. It is for anyone who loves jogging, training in club or training for cross country and marathon. This is the best website to get what they need with different style, brand, color, and mark. Without age limit, sets of requirement, and education criteria, we provide the service and build this website for anyone who enjoy running.
